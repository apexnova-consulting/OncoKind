# Google Search Console Sitemap Submission

1. Sign in to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console).
2. Select the `oncokind.com` property.
3. In the left navigation, open `Sitemaps`.
4. Submit:

   `https://www.oncokind.com/sitemap.xml`

5. Confirm the sitemap status shows `Success`.
6. If the property is not verified yet, verify the domain first, then resubmit the sitemap.

## Recommended follow-up

- Request indexing for:
  - `/trust`
  - `/professional`
  - `/learn`
- Re-submit the sitemap after major content launches, especially when new `/learn/[slug]` pages are added.
